Lead pricing

Lead prices per job tier

A lead's price scales with the value of the job. Bigger jobs cost more per lead but go to fewer pilots — so your investment stays worth something.

Job tierJob valueLead priceExclusivity
Bronze£95 – £199£6 – £12Shared (max 3)
Silver£249 – £549£18 – £30Shared (max 3)
Gold£495 – £2,500£45 – £90Semi-exclusive (max 2)
Platinum£2,500+£120 – £250Exclusive (1 pilot)

Exclusivity protects your conversion. Bronze and Silver leads are shared with up to 3 pilots, Gold with up to 2, and Platinum goes exclusively to 1 pilot. Less competition per lead means a higher chance you win the job — which is why the lead price rises as exclusivity increases.

All prices are indicative and exclude VAT. The final price depends on region, travel time and chosen options. You receive a fixed price per job up front — no surprises afterwards.
