Terms of service

The ground rules for using Skylens. We are a marketplace that brings clients and drone pilots together — we are not ourselves a party to the engagement you enter into with one another.

Draft. This is sample text for illustration only and is not a legally binding agreement. These terms must be reviewed and finalised by a qualified lawyer in the relevant jurisdiction before going live.

Version: draft · 20 June 2026

1. Our role as a marketplace

Skylens provides a platform that connects clients with drone pilots. We facilitate contact, the exchange of information and (where applicable) payment. The drone service itself is delivered by the pilot.

2. Not a party to the contract

The contract to carry out a drone job is formed directly between the client and the pilot. Skylens is not a party to it and bears no responsibility for the performance, quality or delivery of the work. The client and pilot agree price, scheduling, additional work and delivery between themselves.

3. Lead fees, commission & exclusivity

Pilots pay to receive leads and/or a commission on jobs completed through the platform, depending on their membership. The applicable fees, commission rates and exclusivity per job tier are set out on the for pilots page.

  • Leads may be offered shared (multiple pilots), semi-exclusive or exclusive, depending on the tier.
  • A paid lead is not a guarantee of a job; it is access to the request and the contact details.
  • Fees may change; changes are communicated in advance.

All fees and commission charged by Skylens are exclusive of VAT; the applicable VAT rate is 20%.

4. Verification disclaimer

We check pilots for, among other things, Operator ID + Flyer ID (CAA), CAA/EASA certification and insurance, and award a "Verified" label on that basis. This check is a snapshot in time and not a guarantee. Ultimate responsibility for a safe, lawful flight and for complying with all applicable regulations always rests with the pilot. Skylens is not liable for incorrect or out-of-date information provided by a pilot.

5. Footage licence

Rights in the imagery produced are governed by the contract between the client and the pilot. By supplying material for our showcase, the client and pilot grant Skylens a non-exclusive, revocable licence to display that imagery to promote the platform and the pilot concerned. This licence may be withdrawn, after which we will remove the material as soon as reasonably practicable.

6. Liability

Skylens provides the platform "as is" and uses reasonable efforts to keep it working, but does not guarantee uninterrupted availability. To the extent permitted by law, our liability is limited to direct loss and to the amount the relevant party paid us in the preceding twelve months. We are not liable for loss arising from the performance of a job by a pilot or from arrangements between the client and the pilot.

7. Users' obligations

  • You provide accurate and up-to-date information.
  • Pilots hold the required certificates and insurance and keep them current.
  • You do not use the platform for unlawful purposes or to circumvent the rules.
  • You respect the privacy of third parties when capturing and sharing imagery.

8. Changes

We may amend these terms from time to time. We communicate material changes in advance. The most current version is always available on this page.

9. Governing law

These terms and your use of Skylens are governed by English law (Engeland & Wales). Disputes are submitted to the competent courts of that jurisdiction, unless mandatory law provides otherwise.

10. Contact

Questions about these terms? Email hallo@skylens.nl. See also our privacy policy.
